# Memos Plus Promo Video Design

## Style Prompt

Professional, calm, and product-focused. The video should feel like a mature productivity tool for Obsidian power users: structured layouts, clean typography, restrained motion, and clear information hierarchy. Avoid decorative spectacle. Use interface-like panels, Markdown blocks, simple diagrams, and concise captions to explain the product.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F7F5EF`, warm off-white for a quiet workspace feel.
- Primary text: `#202521`, dark neutral with a slight green tone.
- Muted text: `#6F766F`, readable secondary copy.
- Primary accent: `#1F5B50`, deep green for Memos Plus identity and key flows.
- Secondary accent: `#B7613C`, muted copper for highlights and routing markers.
- Line and panel border: `#D9D6CC`, low-contrast structure.
- Soft panel fill: `#FFFFFF`, main UI surfaces.

## Typography

- Primary font stack: `Inter`, `PingFang SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, `Noto Sans CJK SC`, sans-serif.
- Monospace font stack: `JetBrains Mono`, `SFMono-Regular`, `Menlo`, monospace.
- Headings are crisp and compact, not oversized except for the opening product name.
- Chinese body copy should stay short, with natural wrapping and no forced line breaks.

## Motion

- Use measured entrance motion: small vertical shifts, masked reveals, subtle line drawing, and staggered list entries.
- Scene transitions should be clean horizontal wipes or soft panel slides.
- Keep motion slow enough to read. No bouncing, spinning, particle bursts, or chaotic kinetic type.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use busy gradients, decorative blobs, or stock imagery.
- Do not fill the frame with too many feature labels at once.
- Do not use playful or cartoon styling.
- Do not use aggressive zooms or rapid cuts.
- Do not make the video feel like a generic SaaS landing page.
